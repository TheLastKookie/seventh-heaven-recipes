Midgar Special

Description

A delicious Rasberry family special that is sure to leave you wanting more. Created by a special friend's talented mother, it is a home cooked meal that will surly bring a smile to your face. In honor of that special friend, we present this dish. And while it isn't a dish we can make on the regular, it's something special that you can share with loved ones.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 600g Bread Flour
  • 90g Dark Rye Flour
  • 7g Active Dry Yeast
  • 10g Sugar
  • 5g Salt
  • 3g Onion Powder
  • 7g Ground Fennel Seeds
  • 2cups Water
  • 2tbsp Olive Oil

Pizza Sauce

  • 2tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 3 Anchovies
  • 6 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 28oz Whole San Marzano Tomatoes
  • 2tbsp Dried Oregano
  • 2tsp Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Assembly

  • Pepperoni
  • Basil, chopped
  • 32oz Mozzarella Cheese, shredded
  • 9oz Fontina Cheese, shredded
  • 8oz Sharp Cheddar Cheese, shredded

Steps

Pizza Dough

  1. One day before you plan to make the pizza, prepare the dough
  2. Place both flours, yeast, sugar, salt, garlic powder, and ground fennel seeds in a bowl of a stand mixer
  3. In a bowl, combine the water and olive oil
  4. Pour the liquid into the bowl of the stand mixer
  5. Place the bowl in the stand mixer and mix on low w/ a dough hook until all the ingredients come together
  6. When it has formed into a ball, increase the speed of the mixer to medium and knead in the machine for 5 minutes
  7. Lightly flour your counter top and place the kneaded dough there
  8. Smooth the dough into a ball
  9. Spray a large bowl w/ nonstick spay, place the dough ball into the bowl and cover w/ plastic wrap
  10. Place in refrigerator for 18 to 24 hours

Pizza Sauce

  1. Heat a saucepan w. olive oil and butter over medium high heat
  2. Add the anchovies and lightly mash it into small pieces
  3. Add the garlic and cook until the garlic is golden brown, about 2 to 3 minutes
  4. Add the san marzano tomatoes and stir together well
  5. Add the oregano, red pepper flakes, basil, and sugar
  6. Stir well while lightly mashing the whole tomatoes
  7. Reduce the heat to low/medium low and allow the sauce to simmer for about 45 minutes or until thickened while stirring often amd fully mashing the tomatoes by the time it is done cooking
  8. Taste and season w/ salt and pepper to your liking
  9. If prepared the day before, let it cool then store in an airtight container in the refrigerator

Assembly

  1. The next day, remove dough from refrigerator at least 30 minutes before you start to prepare dish
  2. Punch the dough down and place it onto a lightly floured counter top
  3. Split into 4 portions and form into balls, cover with a clean towel and let it rest for 30 minutes
  4. Preheat oven to 475Â°F
  5. Lightly flour your work area and take one of the dough portions and pound into a disk shape
  6. Continue to stretch the dough into a disk shape until it is about the size of a small to medium pizza
  7. Prepare a round baking tray by spraying with a nonstick spray and sprinkling with cornmeal
  8. Place the dough onto the tray and repeat with the other portions
  9. Spread the pizza sauce on top of each dough to your liking
  10. Add a generous layer of the cheeses and top with pepperoni and basil
  11. Finally top with a little more cheese and place the pizza in the oven to bake for 10 minutes
  12. Turn the pizza and then bake for another 10 minutes or until crust has browned nicely
  13. After the pizza has cooked, remove it from the oven and allow it to rest for at least 5 minutes
  14. Slice, plate, and enjoy
