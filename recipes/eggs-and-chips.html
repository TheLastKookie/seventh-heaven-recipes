Eggs and Chips

Description

A staple dish here at Seventh Heaven. With its simple ingredients, its not too hard to make even down here in the Sector 7 Slums. A nice comfort food that is both delicious and filling, especially when made by out hostess Tifa!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Canola Oil
  • Chopped Onions
  • 2lbs Ground Beef (minced)
  • Ground Cumin
  • Chili Powder
  • Cloves Ground
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 Can of Tomato Sauce
  • 1cup Kidney Beans
  • Beef Stock
  • 1 Egg
  • Cheddar and Mozzarella for Serving

Steps

  1. Peel however many potatoes as you like
  2. Cut your peeled potatoes at both ends, and then into fours length wise
  3. Then cut each piece into desired size
  4. Soak the chips in a bowl of water for 30 minutes
  5. Drain the water and dry the chips thoroughly before next step
  6. Deep fry on medium high heat in canola oil
  7. Remove when they tan around the edges
  8. Stir fry chopped onions for a few minutes
  9. Drop in your minced beef
  10. Once the meat browns, season with ground cumin and cloves ground to your liking
  11. Continue stir frying with those spices
  12. Add salt, pepper and any other seasonings you wish
  13. Add the tomato sauce and the kidney beans and mix
  14. Add in a little beef stock and stir and let simmer until liquid thickens a bit
  15. Fry a sunny side up egg and let steam for a bit before plating
  16. Plate the chili and chips and melt some cheese on both to your liking
  17. Finally plate the egg and enjoy
